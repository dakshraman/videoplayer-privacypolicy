# Privacy Policy for Video Player

**Last Updated: April 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Player ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide to Us
- **Private Vault Data:** Any media (images, videos, etc.) you import into the Private Vault is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers.

### b. Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system and version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Information:** We collect information about your activity in the App, such as the features you use and the time spent in the App.

### c. Third-Party Services
Our App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
- **Google AdMob:** Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as your advertising ID and usage patterns to provide personalized ads.
- **Firebase (Google):** Used for analytics and push notifications. Firebase may collect data about your device and how you interact with the App.

## 2. Permissions We Request

To provide our services, we request the following permissions on your device:
- **Storage/Media (Photos, Videos, Audio):** Required to allow you to play local media files and import them into the Private Vault.
- **Camera:** Required if you use features to capture media directly into the Vault.
- **Microphone:** Required for audio recording when capturing videos.
- **Internet Access:** Required for IPTV streaming and fetching advertisements.
- **Notifications:** Required to send you updates and alerts.

## 3. Data Security
The "Private Vault" feature is designed for local privacy. Data moved to the Vault is stored on your device's internal storage and is protected by the password you set. **We do not have access to your password or your Vault files.** If you delete the App or lose your device, Vault data may be permanently lost unless you have a separate backup.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
